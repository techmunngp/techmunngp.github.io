About MUN

WHAT IS MUN (Model United Nations).

Model UN is a simulation of the United Nations (and various other governmental bodies) where participants play the part of representatives meeting in structured committees. While it is an academic activity that requires research and knowledge in world history, economic theory, and political theory, skills like public speaking and negotiation are key.

Every committee in the GA (General Assembly) debates different issues that effect international relations. Every delegate represents their country’s view on the issue. The goal of the UN is to come up with an effective resolution to the issue that passes a majority vote . The best performing delegates are generally awarded at the end of the conference.

Why participate in IEEE Tech MUN ? 
IEEE Tech MUN prides itself on its highly inclusive nature and ability to attract students from all areas of study. It is unique because of the great depth of experiences and perspectives provided by delegates from a wide range of academic backgrounds. This MUN offers students a chance to interactively educate themselves about international problems while increasing their negotiation and public speaking skills. The dynamic peer motivated setting is an ideal place for students to apply their knowledge while creating lasting friendships and having loads of fun.
How do I know that my registration was successful?
You will be redirected to the Townscript page as soon as you click on the Register tab. After you complete you registration and payment process, a ticket will be generated through Townscript which means your registration and payment are successful. You will also receive a confirmation mail on your registered email address.
How many delegates may represent a country? 
One country will be represented by only one delegate. Double delegation is not allowed.
Are there any activities besides the formal council meetings?
Yes there are! Every day after the sessions we have after session drinks and lunch. Apart from that after session drinks, lots of social activities will be organized during Tech MUN for delegates and staff to create a special atmosphere.
Is accommodation and food provided? 
Yes accommodation and food (breakfast, lunch and dinner) will be provided for both days of Tech MUN.
What are the committees and their agendas ?
There are 4 Committees:
UNEP (United Nations Environment Programme)
Creating a framework to prevent and reduce air pollution to improve air quality.
Disarmament and International Security Committee (UNGA DISEC)
Development of Information Technology and Telecommunications in context of International Security.
United Nations Security Council (UNSC)
Analyzing the status of Disputed territories with Special reference to Crimea and South China Sea.
Lok Sabha
Discussing the extent of Section 124(A) (Sedition law) of the Indian Penal Code.
Do I need MUN experience? 
Absolutely not – most of the participants at Tech MUN have no experience with MUN before. Enthusiasm is the characteristic we look for the most.
How do I get to the venue ? 
Transportation facilities will be provided from the accommodation to the venue and for the delegates who live far from the venue in Nagpur. (Opt for transportation during registration).
Is the certificate valuable ?
Yes the the certificate of IEEE Tech MUN carries a great value, since this is an International MUN organized by IEEE Nagpur Subsection.
Are there stipends or financial support for delegates ?
Unfortunately there is no stipend or financial support for delegates. Our budget does not allow us to support delegates’ travel and/or accommodation expenses or to waive their standard fee.
